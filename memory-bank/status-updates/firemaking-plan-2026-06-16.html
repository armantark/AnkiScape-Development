Deferred

Bonfires and fire spirits

Bonfires are historically pre-EoC, but not in the rev-667 source baseline. They will be recorded as a later 2012 extension: batch log feeding, bonus XP, fire-spirit rewards, Cooking boost, and HP warmth need their own design instead of being hidden inside v1 line Firemaking.
