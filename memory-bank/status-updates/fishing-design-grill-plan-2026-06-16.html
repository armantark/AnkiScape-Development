Pre-Implementation Design Grill

Fishing needs one early design decision.

The source-backed 2011Scape data exists locally, but Fishing is structurally different from Mining and Woodcutting: player-facing actions are spot/tool methods that can produce multiple fish, not always one fixed output.

Recommended answer

Use method targets, not fish targets

Implementation branches after this decision
1

Backend: add source-audited Fishing method data, item definitions, pure catch roll logic, storage defaults, review dispatch, achievements, and tests.

2

Frontend: expose Fishing in the Skills hub, render method rows with icons, level and bait/material gating, stable target IDs, and disabled-row click guards.

3

Assets: fetch fish, bait, and fishing tool icons from the registry-derived manifest, record provenance, and cover all required asset paths.

4

Balance: keep base XP from 2011Scape unchanged, treat one successful card as one catch attempt, and model bait consumption separately from XP pacing.
