Why "Pot" needs more than Soft clay

The pottery chain is authentic OSRS: soften clay (a no-XP Utility activity), shape the unfired item, then fire it. Two craft targets, one bank of clay.

Soft clay ×231 ✓ → shape Unfired pot ×0 ✗ → fire Pot

So to actually make Pots: select Unfired pot first (consumes Soft clay), review to stock them, then switch to Pot to fire. The new message points you at the exact next step.
