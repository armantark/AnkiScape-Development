# Fishing 2011Scape Source Audit - 2026-06-16

## Scope Decision

Fishing v1 implements source-backed ordinary Fishing methods bound through the
local 2011Scape rev 667 `FishingSpot.kt` table. Player-facing labels are
output-first, and reusable implements stay implicit in the same spirit as the
current tinderbox/hammer treatment. Only Mining pickaxes and Woodcutting
hatchets have visible progression in the current model.

One successful review runs one scaled Fishing attempt. A dry attempt awards
nothing and consumes no material. A catch awards the rolled fish, Fishing XP,
and any source side XP. Consumable material is consumed only on a successful
catch, matching the local source action.

## Source Mechanics Translation

`Fishing.kt` filters a method's fish list by Fishing level and, for Barbarian
rod catches, by Strength and Agility side levels. It then loops through eligible
fish in source order and rolls each fish's source chance. When any fish succeeds,
the action adds the item, awards Fishing XP, awards side XP where present, and
only then removes one consumable material if the method has one.

AnkiScape keeps those behavioral edges:

- level gates use Fishing level;
- leaping fish also require the source Strength and Agility levels;
- hidden side XP is awarded for Barbarian fish but Strength/Agility do not get
  visible skill surfaces yet;
- material is consumed only on catch;
- no XP, items, or material use happen on dry attempts;
- review scaling adapts source min/max chances to AnkiScape pacing, like Mining
  and Woodcutting, instead of copying game-tick odds directly.

## Utility Bridge

`gather_fishing_bait` is a no-XP Utility/Activities route:

- Display: `Gather fishing bait`
- Requirements: none
- Output: `Fishing bait x28`
- Source: temporary bridge from Lumbridge Fishing Supplies, which stocks Fishing
  bait in the local source.

This is intentionally narrow. It does not add shops, coins, GE behavior,
locations, or a broader bait economy.

## Assets

`tools/fetch_fishing_assets.py` derives its manifest from `fishing_data.py` so
new live rows cannot silently miss icons. It fetches:

- `icon/fishing_icon.png`;
- raw fish and leaping fish icons into `fishingitems/`;
- Fishing bait and material icons needed by live methods;
- `activityicons/gather_fishing_bait.png`.

Reusable Fishing implement art is deliberately not fetched or displayed.

## Deferred And Audit-Only Content

Local source rows that are not live through `FishingSpot.kt` are recorded in
`fishing_data.py` as deferred content rather than exposed in the target list:

- Karambwanji net fishing;
- Rainbow fish fly fishing;
- Oily fishing rod lava eel;
- Lumbridge swamp rod cave eel and frog spawn;
- duplicate barbarian harpoon tuna/swordfish behavior.

The wiki check for the 2011 snapshot to pre-EOC gap found no missing ordinary
Fishing training method that belongs in v1. The notable deltas are audit-only:

- `Stone fish` from the 31 January 2012 Druidic Ritual sparkling fishing spot is
  quest-only and should wait for a quest system.
- Fish Flingers received 2012 improvements, but the D&D itself was released in
  2010 and is a minigame/reward surface, not a normal source-bound catch row.
- Depth Charge Fishing was a 15 November 2012 cosmetic animation override.
- Dungeoneering fish and Fishing Trawler outputs are older omissions from this
  v1 source-binding scope, not post-2011 pre-EOC catch additions.

## Future Follow-Up

Good future slices would be separate source audits for Dungeoneering Fishing,
Fishing Trawler rewards, quest-only fish, or a real shop/GE material economy.
They should not be smuggled into ordinary Fishing v1 because each one needs a
different action model and UI contract.
